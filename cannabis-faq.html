Frequently Asked Questions

What is a medical marijuana card?

A medical marijuana card is a state-issued identification that allows patients with qualifying conditions to purchase and use cannabis legally for medical purposes under the supervision of a licensed physician.

Who qualifies for a medical marijuana card?

Eligibility depends on your medical history and your condition. Common qualifying conditions include chronic pain, anxiety, PTSD, epilepsy, cancer, and more. Our GreenCare physicians will help determine your eligibility during your evaluation.

Can I use my GreenCare certification in another state?

Medical marijuana laws vary by region. Some states allow reciprocity, while others do not. Our team can provide guidance on how to comply with local laws when traveling or relocating.

Do you offer online or virtual consultations?

Yes. GreenCare provides secure virtual evaluations for patients who prefer online appointments. You can complete your consultation from the comfort of your home.
